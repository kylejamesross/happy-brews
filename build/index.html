Good Brew=Happy People

Hello, we’re Happy Brews, your local premium coffee shop. We have the single-minded pursuit of a better cup of coffee. We have travelled the world finding the best coffee beans and custom building our own roaster. This is all in the hopes that the cup of coffee we pour today, will be a bit better then the one served yesterday.

Freshly brewed coffee

All coffee is brewed fresh every 30min. That ensures that you get the best cup of coffee possible. Heck if you are still considered we will freshly brew up a new batch for you!

Skip the line

Order your coffee from our mobile app and skip the line. Additionally, mobile app members gain rewards and advantages!

100% ethical

All our coffee is 100% ethically sourced. We support the farmers growing the coffee. Additionally, we ensure our coffee is free of any synthetic pesticides and herbicides.

Environmentally Friendly

All our coffee cups are 100% recycled and composable. Additionally for every $20 spent we plant one tree for the local community.
